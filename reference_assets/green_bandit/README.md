### Notes

- The downloaded free pack contains the normal green variant only.
- The generated VFX are simple prototype sheets meant to sit closer to the Green Bandit palette and line weight.
- Regenerate the VFX by running `generate_green_bandit_vfx.py`.
- The aligned overlays are currently tuned to the melee attack row and hit row in `normal.png`.
- The gun overlay is a prototype aligned to the most readable firearm-like pose row in `normal.png` (`row1`).
